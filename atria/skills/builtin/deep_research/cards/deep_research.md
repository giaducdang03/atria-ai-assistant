Conduct comprehensive multi-source research on a topic. Generates a MECE taxonomy, then PAUSES and presents it to the user for review — the user can edit the research prompt and regenerate the taxonomy, or accept it and choose a depth level. Once accepted, a background pipeline searches and synthesizes each subtopic, streaming results section by section to the UI. The final report is automatically saved as a Markdown file. Use for broad research requests, analysis reports, or any topic requiring multi-angle coverage. For simple factual questions, use web_search instead.
